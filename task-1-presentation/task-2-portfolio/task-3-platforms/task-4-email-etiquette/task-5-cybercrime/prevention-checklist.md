# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist
### For College Students in India

## General Online Safety
- [ ] Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or password with anyone — not
      even bank officials
- [ ] Enable screen lock and app lock on your phone
- [ ] Use different passwords for different accounts
- [ ] Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on all important accounts
- [ ] Do not click on links received via SMS or WhatsApp from unknown numbers

## UPI / Financial Safety
- [ ] Remember: Scanning a QR code or entering UPI PIN = SENDING money,
      not receiving it
- [ ] Never share your UPI PIN with anyone, ever
- [ ] Always verify the recipient's name before confirming any payment
- [ ] Do not download screen-sharing apps (AnyDesk, TeamViewer) if
      asked by a stranger

## Social Media Safety
- [ ] Keep your phone number and address private on all platforms
- [ ] Do not accept friend requests from unknown people

## If You Are a Victim
- Report immediately at: cybercrime.gov.in
- Call the National Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (24x7, free)
- Also inform your bank within 24 hours to freeze the transaction
