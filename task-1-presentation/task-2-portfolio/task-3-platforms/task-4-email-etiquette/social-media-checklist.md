# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

##  DO's
1. Keep your profiles professional and up to date
2. Use privacy settings to control who sees your posts
3. Think before you post — ask yourself "would I be okay
   if my professor saw this?"
4. Use your college email for academic and professional sign-ups
5. Give credit when sharing someone else's work or ideas

## DON'Ts
1. Don't share your phone number, address, or ID publicly
2. Don't post offensive, aggressive, or inappropriate content
3. Don't accept friend/follow requests from unknown people
4. Don't spread unverified news or misinformation
5. Don't use social media to argue with or disrespect
   professors, classmates, or institutions
